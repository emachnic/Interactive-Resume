The College of Information at Florida State University is one of the top ranked programs in the nation and has held such a ranking for many years. Founded in 1947, the program is old enough to have stability and tradition but young enough to be growing and vital. Our vision statement says it all:

People and Information: Making Vital Connections

The College of Information is proud that, in its more than half-century history, it has produced many graduates who occupy positions of significant leadership and responsibility. We believe that those who are considering enrollment will not have to look far to find someone who can tell them about FSU.
