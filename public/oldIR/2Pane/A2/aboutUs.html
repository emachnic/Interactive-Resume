About Our Company

A.I. Thomas LLC, a Florida Corporation founded in 2003 currently has three corporations under its umbrella: A.I.T. Environmental Technology, Mold Elimination of Florida and All Natural Distributors.

The primary product, Benzyl-Phenol-IAQ, has been tested, according to EPA guidelines, and has been proven to kill over 70 different bacteria, viruses and molds. The application dilution ratio (1:256) renders the product non toxic to humans, animals and plants. Our patented fogging protocol provides an efficient and cost effective method for keeping these pathogens under control.

A.I.T. Environmental Technology provides a revolutionary method to protect against MRSA and other strains of staph bacteria. By using a new proactive protocol developed by AIT, individuals are protected from MRSA. The product is applied in a fog providing more complete coverage and better protection than conventional methods in a fraction of the time required by these other procedures. This fogging procedure may actually eliminate other maintenance requirements and reduce total man hours.

An equally important part of AIT is mold removal and remediation. Being proactive reduces mold liability and the resulting litigation that can affect the real estate business, building contractors, home inspectors, health officials, and home owners. The company provides cost effective services to private homes, condos, apartments, office buildings, schools and universities and military facilities with mold and other indoor air quality issues.
